## My AI Developer Manifesto
1. **Schema-First Thinking:** I believe in designing prompts that guarantee structured outputs (JSON) for seamless integration into real-world applications.
2. **Responsible AI:** I prioritize Guardrails and Contextual Refusals to prevent hallucinations and data leaks in High-Stakes environments.
3. **Continuous Learning:** The AI landscape changes weekly. My goal is to build adaptable systems using techniques like Tree of Thoughts (ToT) and Few-Shot Learning.
